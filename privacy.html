Privacy notice.

Gardn is built by Gardn Labs Limited. This notice explains, plainly, what we collect and why. We’d rather say it clearly than bury it.

What we collect now.

Before launch, the only personal data we hold is the email address you give us to join the list. We use it to send garden notes and to tell you when the app opens. Nothing else.

What we’ll collect in the app.

When Gardn launches, the app will store the garden you build — your plants, borders, photos, care events and your approximate location for weather and seasonal advice. That record is kept to make the app useful to you. It stays yours.

The app and the app stores.

Gardn is coming soon and is not yet downloadable. When it launches, it’s planned for iOS and Android; any data the stores collect on download is governed by their own policies, and we’ll update this notice with the detail before launch.

Your rights.

You can ask us what we hold, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. To remove your email from the list, use the unsubscribe link in any email or see delete account. To reach us, email hello@gardn.world.
