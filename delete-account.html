Delete your data.

Your data is yours. Here’s how to take it back, before launch and after.

Remove your email from the list.

If you’ve joined the list, the quickest way out is the unsubscribe link at the foot of any email we’ve sent — one click and you’re removed. You can also email hello@gardn.world and ask us to delete your address, and we will.

Deleting your account in the app.

Gardn is coming soon. When the app launches, you’ll be able to delete your account and the garden you’ve built — plants, photos, history and all — from within the app’s settings, in a couple of taps. Deletion removes your data from our systems.

If you’d rather we did it.

Email hello@gardn.world from the address on the account and we’ll handle the deletion for you, and confirm when it’s done.
