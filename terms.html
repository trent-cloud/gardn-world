Gardn is a garden app for personal use. You’re responsible for the account you create and for keeping your sign-in details to yourself. Advice in the app is guidance, not a guarantee — gardens, weather and plants don’t always read the manual.

Pricing and trial.

Gardn premium is £5.99 a month, or £49.99 a year — that’s two months free. Every subscription starts with a 7-day free trial, and you won’t be charged if you cancel before it ends.

Free features — including Border Scan, Ask Gardn, health checks, your weekly plan and Bird Listen — don’t require a subscription. Premium features, such as Inspired by Gardn and Inspire imagery, do.

You can cancel anytime. Your subscription runs until the end of the period you’ve paid for, and you keep premium until then.
