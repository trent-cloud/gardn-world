Support

How can we help?

Gardn is coming soon, so there’s not much to fix yet. Here’s what we can answer now, and how to reach a real person.

Before launch.

The app isn’t downloadable yet. The best thing you can do today is join the list — we’ll email you the day it opens, 7-day Premium preview and all. If you’ve already joined and want out, every email we send has an unsubscribe link.

Your data.

If you’ve given us your email for the list, it’s held only to send you garden notes and launch news. You can ask us to remove it at any time — see delete account — and you can read how we handle data in our privacy notice.

Reach us.

Email support@gardn.world and a person will reply. We’re a small team, so it might take a day or two, but it won’t be a bot.
